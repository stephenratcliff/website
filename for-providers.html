The Partnership Model

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Briella Health operates as the full backend. You focus on your clients.

1

You join as a Briella affiliate

Complete a simple onboarding process. You receive a branded co-branded subdomain (e.g., yourmedspa.briellahealth.com), affiliate tracking, and access to your clinic dashboard. No licensing fees or infrastructure costs.

2

Your clients enroll through your branded link

Clients sign up through your branded Briella page. They complete a digital intake form, consent to lab result sharing with your clinic, and receive their panel order automatically. You see every patient who enrolls under your affiliate.

3

Results route directly to your dashboard

When a client's labs are complete, you and your NP are notified in real time. Their full 100+ biomarker panel appears in your clinic dashboard — organized, interpreted, and ready for follow-up consultation. The patient sees their own portal simultaneously.

We handle the regulatory complexity.

Offering lab testing involves CPOM restrictions, HIPAA compliance, and physician oversight. Briella Health manages all of it — so you don't have to.

⚕️

Physician-ordered panels

Every panel is ordered by a licensed physician through Aurora Health's network. You do not need a medical director or NP to place lab orders — that infrastructure is ours.

🔒

HIPAA-compliant throughout

Patient data is handled under a full HIPAA-compliant infrastructure. Patient consent to share results with your clinic is captured at intake. BAAs are in place at every layer of the stack.

Available in TX, PA, NC — expanding

Briella Health is licensed to operate in Texas, Pennsylvania, and North Carolina at launch, with additional states being added through 2026. Ask about your state.
